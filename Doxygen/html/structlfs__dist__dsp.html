Detailed Description

A structure for a multiband distortion object

Parameters
mixparameter for storing amount of distortion for the incoming signal
distortionModparameter for storing the desired distortion mod
saturationparameter for storing saturation for saturation mods 2 (SIG), 4 (TANH), 5 (ATAN) and 6 (FEXP1)
frequencyparameter to store center frequency of bandpass filter
qualityparameter to store q value of the bandpass filter
gainparameter to set gain at center frequency of the bandpass filter
